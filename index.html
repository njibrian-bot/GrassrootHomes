Our Story

Where Craftsmanship
Meets Vision

GrassrootHomes was founded on a singular conviction: that a home should be an enduring expression of the people who inhabit it. From custom new builds to full-scale renovations, our team of architects, designers, and master builders approaches every project with meticulous care.

Headquartered with operations spanning both Canada and the United States, we bring together the finest local craftsmen and globally sourced materials to deliver residences that stand apart — in quality, in beauty, and in character.

What We Do

Our Services

A comprehensive suite of luxury building and renovation services, tailored to your vision.

01 🏠

Custom Home Construction

We build entirely bespoke residences from the ground up. Our architects and builders collaborate closely with you to translate your vision into a meticulously crafted, one-of-a-kind home.

02 🔧

Luxury Renovation

Transform your existing home into a masterpiece. Whether a full-home overhaul or targeted room renovation, we elevate every space with precision craftsmanship and premium materials.

03 🏕

Interior Design

Our in-house interior designers curate every detail — from bespoke millwork and custom cabinetry to lighting schemes and material palettes — ensuring total cohesion throughout.

04 🌀

Landscape & Exterior

First impressions begin at the curb. We design and execute dramatic landscape architecture, driveways, outdoor living areas, and exterior finishing that complement your home perfectly.

05 💡

Smart Home Integration

Modern luxury demands seamless technology. We integrate lighting automation, climate control, security systems, and whole-home audio/visual into every build we deliver.

06 ★

Project Management

From permits and zoning to contractor coordination and final walkthrough, our dedicated project managers ensure your build is on time, on budget, and exceeds expectations.
